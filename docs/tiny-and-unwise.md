# Tiny and unwise

A log of silly ideas with a kernel of wisdom.
The older you get, the less people believe you have created something new.
They assume you remembered it from somewhere and forgot where it came from.
This file is the receipts.

Format: dated entry, the idea, the kernel.

## 2026-05-10

**Tequila Mockingbird.**
Independently created.
A bar name pun on Harper Lee.
Kernel: the canon eats every joke eventually, and the only proof you got there first is to have written it down on the day.

**Careless Whisper is a fart.**
The saxophone riff as flatulence.
Kernel: shame and exposure are baked into the lyric ("guilty feet have got no rhythm"), so the fart reading is structurally consistent. The song is, on a charitable read, *about* a fart.
